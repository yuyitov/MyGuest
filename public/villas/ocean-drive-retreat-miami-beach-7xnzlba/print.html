House info

No smoking inside the apartment. No parties or events. Quiet hours are from 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM. Only registered guests are allowed to stay overnight. Please keep the apartment clean and report any damage as soon as possible. Please rinse off sand before sitting on furniture or beds. Do not flush wipes, feminine products, paper towels, or anything other than toilet paper. Please respect our neighbors and building staff.

Miami Beach is very walkable, but traffic and parking can be busy during weekends and holidays. Bring sunscreen, sunglasses, and comfortable sandals. Many restaurants may add a service charge automatically, so check your bill before adding an extra tip. For the beach, mornings are usually calmer and less crowded. Do not leave valuables unattended at the beach.

Checkout is at 11:00 AM. Before leaving, please: Turn off all lights. Turn off the air conditioning or set it to 76°F. Close and lock all windows and doors. Place used towels in the bathroom. Leave the kitchen tidy. Take out trash if needed. Check that you have all personal belongings. Lock the apartment door behind you. Thank you for staying with us.
