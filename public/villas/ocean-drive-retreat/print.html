Places TO EAT
Larios on the BeachMaps
Cuban classics and fresh mojitos right on the boulevard. Great for people-watching — an Ocean Drive staple.
Joe's Stone CrabMaps
Miami Beach institution since 1913. Stone crabs are a must when in season (Oct–May). Reservations recommended.
Yardbird Southern Table & BarMaps
Beloved Southern comfort food with exceptional fried chicken. Reservations strongly recommended on weekends.
Places TO DRINK
Sweet LibertyMaps
Award-winning craft cocktail bar with an inventive menu and lively crowd. Try the Paper Plane.
The Broken ShakerMaps
Outdoor garden bar in a courtyard setting — one of Miami's most beloved and atmospheric spots.
Things TO DO
South Beach at SunriseMaps
The beach is steps from your door. Early mornings are magical — calm water, cool breeze, and no crowds.
Art Deco Walking TourMaps
Explore Miami Beach's iconic Art Deco architecture on a guided or self-guided tour through the historic district.
Wynwood WallsMaps
World-famous outdoor street art museum, 15 minutes by rideshare. A must-see Miami cultural landmark.
