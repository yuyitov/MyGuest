Questions

Do I need to design anything?

No. Upload what you already have.

Can guests choose the language?

Yes. Every guide includes English, Spanish and French.

Do I get a PDF too?

Yes. You receive a printable PDF version.

Is this only for Airbnb?

No. It works for villas, cabins, guest houses and vacation rentals.

No. You can send your existing welcome book, PDF, Word file, Canva export, photos or screenshots, and fill in only what is missing.

Not yet. During early access, materials are reviewed and set up manually so we can keep the guide accurate and protect private details.

What information is private?

WiFi passwords, access codes and private host contact details are handled through a private guest link, not published on the public page.
